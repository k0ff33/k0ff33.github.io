I have had versions of a personal site before, but this one is meant to stay small and useful: notes about code, coffee, and the personal projects that sit somewhere between the two.

Most of my day-to-day engineering work is around developer platforms, infrastructure, TypeScript systems, and the tooling that makes a team faster without making the stack louder. That is the kind of topic I want to unpack here: the practical bits, the trade-offs, and the things I wish I had written down earlier.

Outside of work, expect the occasional detour into coffee, water sports, home automation, and small experiments that scratch an itch.
