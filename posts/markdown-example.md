This blog should be useful to future me first. If it is useful to someone else, even better.

The shape I want is simple:

- code notes from platform engineering, TypeScript, CI/CD, Terraform, AWS, and monorepo work
- coffee notes that are practical enough to repeat later
- personal project write-ups, especially around home automation and small tools
- short lessons from debugging operational pain and making systems less surprising

The useful posts are usually the ones that start from a real friction point: a slow CI loop, a messy internal API, a leaky abstraction, or a coffee recipe that finally behaves twice in a row.
